User Guide

Welcome to Attendance App! Here’s how to get started:


1) Create a user account.

2) Once you're logged in, click on the "Rosters" tab to create a roster for each class you teach.

3) Click on the "Create a Student" tab to set up your students’ individual profiles with their names, phone numbers, and any important notes about them (such as allergies).

4) Under the "Rosters" tab, click on any roster to add your students for that class.

5) Also under the "Rosters" tab, create a session each time a class meets by inputting the date, start and end times.

6) Mark students’ attendance for that class!


Other Tips
  • Need to view a student's entire attendance record? Just click on their name in your student list to view their profile.
  • A student's profile can be edited! Just open it up and click on the "edit" link below the notes field.
